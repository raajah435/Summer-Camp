Welcome to our STEM Summer Robotics Camp, where we combine the exciting world of robotics with hands-on exploration in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). This summer, we invite young innovators to embark on a journey of discovery, creativity, and problem-solving through immersive experiences designed to ignite their passion for STEM fields.

Our camp aims to:

Introduce campers to the fundamentals of robotics and STEM concepts. Foster critical thinking, collaboration, and creativity in a supportive learning environment. Provide hands-on experience with robotics kits, programming, and engineering principles. Inspire curiosity and a lifelong love for STEM disciplines.

Kit-based Learning
(Coding, Electronics, Robotics)

  • Age: 6 to 8, 9 to 12, 13+
  • Focus Areas:Coding (Scratch, Python), Electronics (basic circuits, sensors), and Robotics (building and programming robots).

Drones and Aero Modelling

  • Duration: 10 days

Satellite Technology for Kids

  • Duration: 5 days

AI and ML for Kids

  • Duration: 15 days
  • Age: 13+

Graphic Designing and 3D Printing

  • Duration: 10 days
